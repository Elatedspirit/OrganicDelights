There are two ways to make Apple-Celery Stuffing

1.Apple Stuffing with Celery, Herbs, and Onions:

Ingredients:

  • 140g softened butter
  • 5-6 ribs of celery (about 2 cups), chopped
  • 2 large crisp apples (such as Pink Lady, Honeycrisp, or Macoun), diced (leave the peel on)
  • 2 large fresh bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh herbs (parsley, sage, rosemary, and thyme)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 6 cups homemade croutons (from stale bread) or 1 bag of sage and onion stuffing cubes (e.g., Pepperidge Farm brand)
  • 1 tablespoon Bell‚Äôs ground poultry seasoning
  • 1 quart chicken or turkey stock

Instructions:

  • Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F.
  • Melt 6 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saut√© the celery, onion, apples, bay leaves, herbs, salt, and pepper until softened (about 10-12 minutes).
  • Add the cubed bread and season with Bell‚Äôs poultry seasoning. Moisten with chicken or turkey stock and stir to absorb.
  • Butter a casserole dish with the remaining butter and fill it with the stuffing mixture. Dot the top with the rest of the butter.
  • Roast the stuffing for 30-40 minutes until crispy at the edges.

2.Easy Apple Stuffing for Two:

Ingredients:

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 apple, diced
  • 2-3 celery ribs, chopped
  • Poultry seasoning, salt, and pepper
  • Bread cubes (about 2 cups)
  • Chicken broth

Instructions:

  • Melt the butter in a skillet. Add the onion, diced apple, and celery. Cook until softened (4 to 6 minutes).
  • Stir in poultry seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  • Mix in the bread cubes and pour chicken broth over the top. Stir to coat
  • Transfer the mixture to a prepared baking dish and bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the top is toasted and crunchy.
