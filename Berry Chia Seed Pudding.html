There are two ways to make Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

1.Mediterranean Quinoa Salad by Love and Lemons:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • Roasted cherry tomatoes (for rich flavor)
  • Persian cucumbers (for crunch)
  • Red onion (sharp depth of flavor)
  • Feta cheese (creamy texture and tangy flavor; skip if vegan)
  • Kalamata olives (briny and salty)
  • Toasted pine nuts and roasted chickpeas (protein and crunch)
  • Arugula and mixed herbs (freshness)
  • Italian dressing (bright and tangy)

Instructions:

  • Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  • Drizzle with Italian dressing and toss to combine.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  • Top with roasted chickpeas and serve.
A hearty and flavorful quinoa salad with roasted tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, feta cheese, and fresh herbs. This easy recipe is perfect for summer cookouts, picnics, or healthy lunches. You can customize it with your favorite ingredients and enjoy it as a side dish or a main event.
