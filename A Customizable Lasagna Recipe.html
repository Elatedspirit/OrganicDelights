two ways to make Customizable Lasagna Recipe

1. Homemade Lasagna:

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets (no-boil)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Lean ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pasta sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley
  • Egg

Instructions:

  • Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and Italian sausage. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  • Stir in the pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, and fresh parsley. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with an egg.
  • Layer the lasagna: Start with a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella, and more meat sauce. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used up.
  • Top with additional shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  • Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

2. Deconstructed Lasagna (Lazy Lasagna in One Pot):

Ingredients:

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Egg and ricotta mixture
  • Marinara sauce
  • Lasagna noodles (broken into pieces)
  • Ground pork (or Italian sausage)
  • Ground beef

Instructions:

  • In a deep-sided pan, brown the ground beef and ground pork (or Italian sausage).
  • Stir in marinara sauce and broken lasagna noodles. Let it simmer until the noodles are cooked.
  • In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg.
  • Serve the deconstructed lasagna with dollops of ricotta mixture and shredded mozzarella on top.
